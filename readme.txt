WooCommerce integration for Shop1.

== Description ==
Shop1 provides an ever-growing product catalog of all quality and vetted US-based dropshipping products.
Quickly browse through, find the best products, and add them to your WooCommerce store with 1-click.
From new brands to the most established ones, you can find them all from your Shop1 dashboard.

= Access to thousands of dropshipping products =
All products come with quality pictures, descriptions, discounted pricing, shipping costs, and specifications.

= Variety of dropshipping niches =
Discover and sell products from every category, including clothing, jewelry, fragrance, toys, educational supplies, health & beauty, CBD, sports, workout supplements, furniture & decor, pet goods, electronics, safety, and adult products.

= Order processing automation =
Shop1 automatically handles your orders' communications to the suppliers and sends the tracking information back to WooCommerce Shipment Tracking and your order's internal notes.

= Catch trends quickly =
Use Shop1's proprietary filters to discover new trends and hot-selling products for the season. Stay up-to-date with the latest industry news, and utilize our Staff Picks to prepare for the coming seasonal product trends.

== Installation ==
1. This is a WooCommerce extension. So first install and setup [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/).
2. Install and activate the plugin from wordpress admin plugins screen.
3. Connect the plugin to Shop1 store by visiting configure screen of the plugin.
4. Sync your products and orders.
